About GT CAR

GT cars, also known as Grand Touring cars or Gran Turismo cars, are a type of high-performance luxury sports cars designed for long-distance driving in comfort and style. The term "Grand Touring" originated in Europe in the early 20th century and referred to the tradition of taking long journeys or tours across different countries.
